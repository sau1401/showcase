daily tea thoughts

What is this?

This is just random tea documentation notes. I'm really into tea so all I do is write about the tea I've had that day and figure out which is the best way to brew it. I also review the tea sometimes (on a scale from 0-10), and determine if certain brewing times are "better" than others. Sometimes, I might neglect to care, and others I won't, it's entirely dependent on how I feel. And as something extra, I will include links to the websites that host the teas I have.
